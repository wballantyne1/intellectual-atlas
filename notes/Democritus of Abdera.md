`thinker` | c. 460–370 BCE | Greek
**Traditions:** Pre-Socratic Philosophy · Atomism · Materialism

## Summary
The originator of atomic theory: reality consists of indivisible particles (atoms) moving through void. Everything — including thought and soul — is material. His theory was ignored for two millennia, then became the foundation of modern chemistry and physics. One of the most prescient thinkers in history.
