# Ma'at
`idea` | First articulated: c. 2700 BCE | Contested: no
**Traditions:** Egyptian Wisdom Tradition · Ethics · Cosmology
**Primary tradition:** [[Egyptian Wisdom Tradition]]

---

## Summary
The ancient Egyptian concept of cosmic order, truth, justice, and harmony. Both a description of how the universe is and a moral demand on how humans must act within it. One of the earliest known attempts to articulate a universal, impersonal principle governing both nature and human conduct.
