# Epictetus
`thinker` | c. 50–135 CE | Greek (Phrygian-Roman)
**Traditions:** Stoicism · Roman Philosophy

> **Stub** — full node not yet built

## Summary
Born a slave, became one of the most influential Stoic teachers. His *Discourses* and *Enchiridion* (Handbook) are the most practically focused Stoic texts. The opening of the *Enchiridion* — the distinction between what is and isn't 'up to us' — is the core of Stoic practice. Marcus Aurelius studied him closely.
