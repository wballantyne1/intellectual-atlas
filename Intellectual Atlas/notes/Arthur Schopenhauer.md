# Arthur Schopenhauer
`thinker` | 1788–1860 | German
**Traditions:** German Idealism · Pessimism · Voluntarism

> **Stub** — full node not yet built

## Summary
Took Kant's framework and radicalised it: if the thing-in-itself is unknowable through reason, Schopenhauer argued we have one privileged access point — our own bodies, which reveal the thing-in-itself as blind, striving Will. His pessimism and aesthetics influenced Wagner, Nietzsche, and Freud.
