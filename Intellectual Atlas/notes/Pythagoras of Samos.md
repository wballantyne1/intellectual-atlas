# Pythagoras of Samos
`thinker` | c. 570–495 BCE | Greek (Samian)
**Traditions:** Pre-Socratic Philosophy · Mathematical Philosophy · Pythagorean School

---

## Summary
Proposed that fundamental reality is number — not a physical substance but mathematical ratio and proportion. The discovery that musical harmonies correspond to simple numerical ratios led him to generalise: reality is mathematical at its core. His influence on Plato was decisive, and through Plato on all of Western thought.
