# Marcus Aurelius
`thinker` | 121–180 CE | Roman
**Traditions:** Stoicism · Roman Philosophy

---

## Summary
Roman Emperor and Stoic philosopher — the most powerful person in the world who wrote one of the most intimate philosophical documents in history. The *Meditations* are private notes to himself: reminders to practise Stoic principles under the pressure of empire, war, and plague. One of the most widely read philosophical texts in the world today.
