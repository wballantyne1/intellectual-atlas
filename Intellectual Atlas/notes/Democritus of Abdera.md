# Democritus of Abdera
`thinker` | c. 460–370 BCE | Greek (Thracian)
**Traditions:** Pre-Socratic Philosophy · Atomism · Materialism

---

## Summary
Originator of atomic theory: all reality consists of indivisible particles moving through void. Everything — matter, mind, soul — is composed of atoms of different shapes and sizes. Ignored for two millennia, vindicated by modern physics. His response to Parmenides is one of the most elegant moves in ancient philosophy: accepted Parmenidean atoms, but admitted the void to rescue change.
