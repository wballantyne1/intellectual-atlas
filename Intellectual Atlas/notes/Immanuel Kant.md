# Immanuel Kant
`thinker` | 1724–1804 | Prussian (German)
**Traditions:** German Idealism · Enlightenment · Critical Philosophy

---

## Summary
Arguably the pivot of modern Western philosophy. Kant argued that the human mind actively shapes experience rather than passively receiving it — space, time, and causality are frameworks we impose on the world, not features we read off it. His 'Copernican revolution' attempted to reconcile the scientific rationalism of Newton with the scepticism of Hume.
