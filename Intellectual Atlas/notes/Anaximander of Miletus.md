# Anaximander of Miletus
`thinker` | c. 610–546 BCE | Greek (Milesian)
**Traditions:** Pre-Socratic Philosophy · Milesian School · Natural Philosophy

---

## Summary
Student of Thales and the first philosopher to give a genuine philosophical *argument* rather than merely assert a position. He rejected water as the arche by asking: if water is fundamental, what is water itself made of? His answer — the Apeiron — was the first fully abstract concept in Western philosophy.
