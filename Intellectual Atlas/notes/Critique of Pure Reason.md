# Critique of Pure Reason
`idea` | 1781 | Contested: no
**Traditions:** Critical Philosophy · Epistemology · Metaphysics
**Primary thinker:** [[Immanuel Kant]]

---

## Summary
Kant's 1781 masterwork and one of the most consequential books in Western philosophy. It asks: what can human reason know, and what are its limits? Kant drew a hard boundary between legitimate scientific knowledge and the overreaching claims of traditional metaphysics — about God, the soul, and the universe as a whole.
