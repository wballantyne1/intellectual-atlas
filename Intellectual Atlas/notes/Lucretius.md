# Lucretius
`thinker` | c. 99–55 BCE | Roman
**Traditions:** Epicureanism · Roman Philosophy · Poetry

---

## Summary
Roman poet whose single work, *De Rerum Natura*, preserved atomic theory for Western civilisation. Lost for a thousand years, rediscovered in 1417 — its reappearance helped spark the Renaissance and the Scientific Revolution. Influenced Galileo, Newton, and Jefferson.

---

## Influenced by
- [[Epicurus]] — faithful transmitter of Epicurean physics and ethics
- [[Democritus of Abdera]] — atomism as the foundation

## Ideas
- [[Atomism]] — the fullest poetic account of ancient atomic theory
- [[Ataraxia]] — freedom from fear as the goal

## Influenced
- The Renaissance and Scientific Revolution (via rediscovery 1417)
