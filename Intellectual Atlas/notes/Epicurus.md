# Epicurus
`thinker` | 341–270 BCE | Greek (Samian)
**Traditions:** Epicureanism · Hellenistic Philosophy · Materialism

---

## Summary
Founder of Epicureanism — one of history's most misunderstood philosophies. Not hedonistic excess but tranquil simplicity: friendship, philosophical conversation, freedom from fear. He used Democritus's atomism to dissolve the two great human anxieties — fear of death and fear of divine punishment. His Garden admitted women and slaves, a radical departure for the ancient world.
