# Chrysippus
`thinker` | c. 279–206 BCE | Greek (Cilician)
**Traditions:** Stoicism · Logic

> **Stub** — full node not yet built

## Summary
The third head of the Stoic school and its greatest systematic philosopher. Said to have saved Stoicism with his arguments. Developed Stoic logic (propositional logic) far beyond the syllogistic of Aristotle — a development largely ignored until the 20th century.
