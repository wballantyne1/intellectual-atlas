# Logos
`idea` | First articulated: c. 500 BCE | Contested: yes
**Traditions:** Pre-Socratic Philosophy · Stoicism · Christian Theology · Neoplatonism
**Primary thinker:** [[Heraclitus of Ephesus]]

---

## Summary
The rational principle or word that orders the cosmos. In Heraclitus, the hidden structure beneath all flux. Taken up by the Stoics as the active rational principle pervading all matter. Fused with Jewish theology by Philo of Alexandria. Identified with Christ in the Gospel of John. One of the most-travelled ideas in intellectual history.
