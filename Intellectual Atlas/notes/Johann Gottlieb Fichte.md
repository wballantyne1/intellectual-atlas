# Johann Gottlieb Fichte
`thinker` | 1762–1814 | German
**Traditions:** German Idealism

> **Stub** — full node not yet built

## Summary
The first major post-Kantian idealist. Fichte argued that Kant's "thing-in-itself" was an incoherent remnant and sought to derive all reality from the self-positing activity of the "I". His work was the bridge between Kant and Hegel.
