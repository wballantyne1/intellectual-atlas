# Emanation
`idea` | First articulated: c. 253 CE | Contested: yes
**Traditions:** Neoplatonism · Metaphysics · Cosmology
**Primary thinker:** [[Plotinus]]

---

## Summary
The Neoplatonic account of how the world comes to exist: not through deliberate creation but through necessary overflow. The One is so full of reality that it overflows into lesser levels — Nous, Soul, Matter — as the sun overflows into light without any act of will. Reality is a hierarchy of levels, each less unified than the one above.

---

## Connections
- Proposed by: [[Plotinus]]
- Works through: [[The One]] → Nous → Soul → Matter
- Draws on: [[Theory of Forms]] — Nous is the realm of Forms
- Contrasts with: Christian creation ex nihilo — emanation is necessary, not willed
- Transmitted via: [[Islamic Golden Age Tradition]] — Al-Farabi, Avicenna adopted and transformed it
