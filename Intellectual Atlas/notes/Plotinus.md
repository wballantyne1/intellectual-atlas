# Plotinus
`thinker` | c. 204–270 CE | Egyptian-Roman
**Traditions:** Neoplatonism · Mysticism · Late Antique Philosophy

---

## Summary
Founder of Neoplatonism. Born in Egypt, educated in Alexandria, taught in Rome. Fused Plato's philosophy with mystical and Oriental elements into a grand vision: all reality emanates from a single source — the One — through successive levels down to matter. Became the philosophical backbone of both Christian theology and Islamic philosophy.
