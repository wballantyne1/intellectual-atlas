# Thales of Miletus
`thinker` | c. 624–546 BCE | Greek (Milesian)
**Traditions:** Pre-Socratic Philosophy · Milesian School · Natural Philosophy

---

## Summary
Traditionally the first philosopher in the Western tradition. Thales asked what the world is fundamentally made of and answered: water. The answer matters less than the move — this was the first known attempt to explain the cosmos through a single natural principle rather than through myth or divine agency.
