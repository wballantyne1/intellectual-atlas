# Zeno of Citium
`thinker` | c. 334–262 BCE | Cypriot-Greek (Athenian)
**Traditions:** Stoicism · Hellenistic Philosophy

---

## Summary
Founder of Stoicism. Began teaching in the Stoa Poikile ('Painted Porch') in Athens around 300 BCE, giving the school its name. Synthesised Heraclitus's Logos with practical ethics: if the universe is governed by rational law, the good life consists in living in accordance with it. Born of Phoenician descent — one of the first major philosophers with clear non-Greek origins.
