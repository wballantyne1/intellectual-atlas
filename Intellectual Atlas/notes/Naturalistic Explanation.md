# Naturalistic Explanation
`idea` | First articulated: c. 585 BCE | Contested: no
**Traditions:** Pre-Socratic Philosophy · Philosophy of Science
**Primary thinker:** [[Thales of Miletus]]

---

## Summary
The move from mythological to natural explanations — accounting for the world through impersonal causes and principles rather than divine will or narrative. The founding gesture of Western philosophy and science.
