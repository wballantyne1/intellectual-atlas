# The One
`idea` | First articulated: c. 253 CE | Contested: yes
**Traditions:** Neoplatonism · Metaphysics · Mysticism
**Primary thinker:** [[Plotinus]]

---

## Summary
Plotinus's absolute first principle — utterly simple, beyond being, beyond thought, beyond description. All reality emanates from the One as light from the sun. Not a god who creates willfully, but a principle whose very nature overflows into existence. Became the philosophical backbone of mystical theology in both Christianity and Islam.

---

## Connections
- Proposed by: [[Plotinus]]
- Develops: [[Theory of Forms]] — the Form of the Good radicalised into absolute simplicity
- Generates (via [[Emanation]]): Nous → Soul → Matter

- Influenced: Islamic Sufi mysticism, Jewish Kabbalah
