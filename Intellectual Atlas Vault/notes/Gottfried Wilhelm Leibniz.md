# Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz
`thinker` | 1646–1716 | German
**Traditions:** Rationalism · Metaphysics · Mathematics

> **Stub** — full node not yet built

## Summary
One of the great rationalist philosophers, and along with Newton the co-inventor of calculus. His metaphysics — monads, pre-established harmony, the principle of sufficient reason — formed the dominant philosophical system in German universities that Kant was trained in and eventually overturned.
