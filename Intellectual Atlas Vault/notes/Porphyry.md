# Porphyry
`thinker` | c. 234–305 CE | Phoenician-Greek
**Traditions:** Neoplatonism · Logic

> **Stub** — full node not yet built

## Summary
Student of Plotinus and editor of the *Enneads*. His *Isagoge* (Introduction to Aristotle's *Categories*) was the most widely read logical text in the medieval world, transmitted to both Islamic and Christian scholastic traditions. His editorial work preserved Plotinus for posterity.
